Packaging design

I create packaging that blends brand identity, visual impact, and functionality. Throughout my career, I have designed packaging for global brands such as Mattel, Kodak, and Daewoo, as well as for the Korean and Argentine cosmetics sector, meeting high standards of quality and aesthetics. Each project is crafted to reflect the essence of the product, stand out in the market, and build an emotional connection with consumers through strategic and memorable design.
