Performance Marketing Strategy – Concept Project

This strategic case study was developed as part of a marketing assignment for an interview process. The brief involved creating a full-funnel performance strategy for the launch of Charles & Keith’s Capsule Collection in APAC.

While this is not a real campaign, the proposal showcases my ability to analyze digital platforms, define targeting strategies, allocate media budgets, and build data-driven marketing plans tailored to mobile-first markets like Singapore, Malaysia, and Indonesia.

Key areas included:

  • ➔ Website performance audit & UX improvements
  • ➔ Google Ads & Meta campaign structure
  • ➔ Audience segmentation based on behavior and intent
  • ➔ Budget distribution by country and funnel stage
  • ➔ Forecasting, KPIs, and reporting structure
